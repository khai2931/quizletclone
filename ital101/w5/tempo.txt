// weather forecast
weather forecast				previsioni mateo
what is the weather like?		che tempo fa?
it is nice|bad out				e\ bello|brutto
the weather is nice|bad			fa bel|brutto tempo
the weather is dreadful			il tempo e\ pessimo
what is the temp?				quanti gradi ci sono?
it is 18 degrees out			ci sono 18 gradi
